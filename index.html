About the Event

The Aeromodelling Club at NIT Kurukshetra is excited to present an event showcasing the world of remote-controlled (RC) planes and drones.
Join us for a day filled with excitement, learning, and fun!

About the Club
  • Introduction to Aeromodelling: Learn the basics of aerodynamics and how to design and build RC planes and drones.
  • RC Plane Flying:Thrilling demonstrations of RC planes in action, showcasing various maneuvers and capabilities.
  • Workshops and Demonstrations: Engage in interactive workshops and demonstrations led by experienced aeromodellers, covering topics such as flight controls, propulsion systems, and aerial photography.
Aeromodelling Club Presents
  • Drone Racing Competition: Participate in an exhilarating drone racing competition where pilots showcase their skills in navigating obstacle courses.
  • DIY Drone Building: Build your own mini drone with guidance from seniors.
  • Aeromodelling Quiz: Test your knowledge about aeromodelling and win exciting prizes.
  • Paintball Event: Engage in Adrenaline-pumping battles,showcasing both strategy and skill
